Getting Started

Our typical implementation timeline is 2-4 weeks depending on the complexity of your business and current accounting setup.

Week 1: Initial consultation, data gathering, and system setup

Week 2-3: Data migration, process configuration, and testing

Week 4: Final review, training, and go-live

To begin our partnership, we'll need access to the following:

  • Financial Records: Recent financial statements, trial balance, and chart of accounts
